My Story

From curious tinkerer to professional developer, here's a little bit about my journey.

Who I Am

My journey into technology began with a fascination for how things work, which led me to disassemble (and occasionally reassemble) old electronics. This curiosity naturally evolved into a passion for software development, where I could build entire worlds with just a few lines of code.

I pursued a degree in Computer Science, where I built a strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and software design principles. During my studies, I worked on numerous projects, from simple mobile apps to complex database systems, which solidified my love for creating practical and user-centric solutions.

My Philosophy

I believe in the power of clean code, continuous learning, and collaborative problem-solving. For me, development is not just about writing functional code, but about crafting elegant, efficient, and maintainable solutions that stand the test of time. I thrive in environments where I can work with a team to tackle challenging problems and build products that make a real impact.
